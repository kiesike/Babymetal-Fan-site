SU-METAL

Suzuka Nakamoto

The main vocalist and central voice of BABYMETAL, known for clean power, control, and a stage presence that cuts through the heaviest arrangements.

  • Role: Vocal and Dance
  • Known for: Lead vocals, dramatic command, and emotional delivery
  • Group status: Current member

Profile

Suzuka Nakamoto performs as SU-METAL. Her voice gives BABYMETAL its melodic center, balancing theatrical metal production with polished pop phrasing.

Stage Role

She anchors the live show as the front vocalist, often carrying the emotional weight of songs while the choreography builds around her.
