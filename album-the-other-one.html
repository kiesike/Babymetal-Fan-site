Concept Album

The Other One

A darker concept-driven release connected to the restoration of the Metalverse and the band's transition into a new phase. It feels more cinematic, reflective, and world-built than the earlier albums.

  • Release era: 2023
  • Style: Cinematic metal, electronic atmosphere, mature production
  • Best for: The serious, world-building side of BABYMETAL
  • Era note: The restoration project and the doorway into the current trio

Highlighted Tracks

  • METAL KINGDOM
  • Monochrome
  • Divine Attack
  • Mirror Mirror

Why It Matters

The Other One refined the band's storytelling and prepared the ground for the current trio era that followed.
