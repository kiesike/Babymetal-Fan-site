Debut Album

BABYMETAL

The first full-length statement: chaotic, catchy, theatrical, and the release that introduced BABYMETAL's genre collision to a wider audience. It is the blueprint for kawaii metal as a global talking point.

  • Release era: 2014
  • Style: Kawaii metal, J-pop hooks, thrash and power metal influences
  • Best for: Understanding the origin of the BABYMETAL formula
  • Era note: Viral discovery, early lore, and the rise of "Gimme Chocolate!!"

Highlighted Tracks

  • Gimme Chocolate!!
  • Megitsune
  • Ijime, Dame, Zettai
  • Headbangeeeeerrrrr!!!!!

Why It Matters

This album set the rules for the project: idol melody can sit beside blast beats, arena chants, and guitar heroics without apologizing for the contrast.
