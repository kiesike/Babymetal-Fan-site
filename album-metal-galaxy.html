Metal Galaxy

A sprawling trip through the Metalverse, blending BABYMETAL's core sound with international influences, guest-heavy experimentation, and a bigger global concept.

  • Style: Global metal fusion, pop-metal, electronic textures
  • Best for: Variety, spectacle, and genre-hopping ambition
  • Era note: The Forum, global guests, and the first major post-YUIMETAL album

Highlighted Tracks

Why It Matters

This album widened BABYMETAL's universe, presenting the group as a border-crossing act that could make metal feel colorful, theatrical, and global.
