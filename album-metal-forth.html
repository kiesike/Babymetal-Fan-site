Metal Forth

The modern collaboration era, built around BABYMETAL's global network and the first original album with MOMOMETAL as an official member. It pushes the band into a louder, more connected worldwide phase.

  • Release era: 2025
  • Style: Collaborative global metal, electronicore, deathcore, pop-metal
  • Best for: The newest chapter of BABYMETAL's worldwide identity
  • Era note: Billboard 200 Top 10, Capitol Records, and cross-scene collaborations

Highlighted Tracks

  • RATATATA with Electric Callboy
  • Song 3 with Slaughter to Prevail
  • from me to u featuring Poppy
  • METALI!! featuring Tom Morello

Why It Matters

Metal Forth turns BABYMETAL's worldwide friendships into the album concept, showing the group moving beyond one scene and into a shared global metal language.
